Our Social Network

Department of general medicine
The medicine department caters to patient's routine illnesses and specifically to cardiac conditions. With a day care facility, patients can be admitted for day care intravenous medications. The department has a ECG and a TMT unit for cardiac evaluation.
Department of pathology and micro biology
The department caters to all kinds of routine blood and urine sampling and investigations. Our separate window for maxillofacial patients and attractive pre surgical packages is a hit amongst practioners. The department also has a histopathologic unit dealing with all kinds of biopsies.
Department of maxillofacial radiology and medicine
This department focuses on cbct scans and digital opg scans. The cbct scans are a gold standard investigation of choice for bony pathologies and maxillofacial trauma. Also, the cbct is a must requirement before dental implant placement. The department caters to implant planning of all companies and takes pride in quick and efficient online reports to the doctors as well as patients. The oral medicine department deals with management or oral cancers, precancers, oral ulcerative conditions, tobacco cessation and TMJ disorders.

Panoramic Imaging

This gives an overview of the entire dentition in a single scan. With the digital accuracy, it helps in diagnosis of subtle bony changes to massive expansile lesions.

Panoramic Imaging

The 2D TMJ imaging or the lateral TMJ panorama gives an accurate account of the range of motion of the bilateral jaws and helps in diagnosis of bony pathologies of the same.

Cephalometry

The Lateral cephalogram give excellent soft and hard tissue differentiation. Pre, intermediate and post orthodontic scans with cephalometric analysis helps in accurate patient management and saves time for the orthodontist.

Cephalometry

With this latest addition, we can give skull views for an overview and scan of entire maxillo-facial region for diagnosis of facial asymmetry, zygomatic arch fractures.

Cephalometry

The PNS view gives a detailed description of the sinuses. The entire anatomy of the sinuses with the nasal cavity and the septum can be viewed in this.

Cephalometry

The hand wrist radiograph can help in age estimation as well the development of skeletal maturity.

CBCT imaging

Implant assessment and analysis : With an advanced software analysis, the pre and the post implant assessment of the maxillofacial region can be achieved. We can provide the bone density as well as the volume of graft material needed for sinus lifts.

CBCT imaging

Assessment of impacted teeth and mixed dentition analysis: To localize the position of impacted teeth- multiple or single and the locate the third molar nerve relations, CBCT is an excellent mode to guide the clinician saving time and excessive trauma to the patient.
Mixed dentition posses a challenge as when to extract or whether to extract or not- A single scan provides the exact status of the tooth root length and developing dentition.

Maxillofacial trauma

Mid face trauma or Lefort fractures can be a challenge in 2-d radiography. A CBCT can provide a resolution of 180 micro meters sufficient to rule out green stick fractures also.

Maxillofacial lesions

To assess smaller lesions with area or volumetric analysis or to record the entire extent of a larger maxillofacial lesion with proximity to the vital structures â€“ CBCT scan can be your investigation of choice.
With advanced available analysis, the maxillofacial clefts are analysed much better.

Airway Analysis

Sleep apnoea is a disease related to modern days. Obesity and sleep apnoea are directly related. The analysis of the airway can give the A-P dimensions and the cross section of the airway to rule out restrictive diseases.

Endodontic analysis

The advance analysis can accurately predict fractures, root canal length and pulpal anatomy. The scan can give us answers for the reason of endodontic failures and also guide in re treatment cases.

Superimposition studies

This can serve as a prognostic indicator of treatment. The pre and post scans can be super imposed to find out the treatment outcome especially in cases of Mucor mycosis or osteomyelitis.

TMJ imaging

A 3d scan of the open and closed mouth TMJ can shows us the changes especially wrt the range of motion of the condyles.
